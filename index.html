Jan 5th 6:18
As I write this
During a late-night drive
I find these moments precious
With you
In a limited space and time
I wish I could stop looking at the road
And look at you
All this flat land
Reminds me of the Netherlands
My wanders to a distant future
What if we see a future?
Together.
I realize I will never completely know you
A playlist called weeee
Driving at the age of 19
But that’s beautiful
And I never want that to stop
And hopefully one day I will truly know you

Pillow Talk (Jan 27th 2026)
My room at Found is bleak
I never decorated it because I thought
“What’s the use if I am to leave?”
Thus, ornaments and trinkets I never bought

But I was always envious of room 943
A space of comfort in a foreign city
A sanctuary from all this noise
Though I was grateful I did not have her roomie

It smelled like spices
It smelled like joy
It smelled like laughter
Now it smells like a good memory
It felt like home
Obviously, not because of my lover
Ok
Maybe it is

Now her room has been invaded
I will likely never step in there again
I wonder if it smells the same
Selfishly, I hope it does not

Now I am in my room
Wondering
“What is she up to?”
A little daydream to help me cope
I almost bring myself upstairs
Hoping she still resides there
But I give myself the bliss of memory

My room remains bleak
But there is now a new color scheme
Of matte pink
A pink pillow on my bed

It makes me like my room a little more
I think about you when I lay my head
Though my intentions aren’t always so pure
